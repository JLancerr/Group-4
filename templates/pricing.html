Frequently Asked Questions

Do I Retain Ownership of My Content?

Of course! Your content is 100% yours and will remain so, as well as all your student data. We will not contact your students or market our products to them.

Can I Create a Free Online Course?

Absolutely! Free courses make for ideal lead magnets. You can offer, for example, a beginners course for free, and then at the end you can talk about your other premium courses.

Can I Change My Plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time, and cancel anytime as well. There are no signed contracts or long-term commitments.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

Upon cancellation, premium features remain accessible until the current billing period ends. Afterward, your account reverts to the free tier.

Does the subscription work across devices?

Yes, subscriptions generally sync across mobile, tablet, and desktop versions of the platform.

What happens if I forget to renew my subscription?

If you don’t renew, your account typically reverts to the free tier, but your data remains intact.
